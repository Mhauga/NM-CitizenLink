Executive Branch

The executive branch, led by Governor Michelle Grisham, is responsible for implementing and enforcing state laws. This branch includes the Lieutenant Governor, Attorney General, Secretary of State, and many more!

Legislative Branch

The legislative branch, consisting of the New Mexico House of Representatives and the New Mexico Senate, which together form the New Mexico Legislature, is tasked with making state laws.

Judicial Branch

The judicial branch, headed by the New Mexico Supreme Court, interprets laws and administers justice. This branch includes the lower courts, and other judicial bodies that hear cases related to state laws and the state constitution.
